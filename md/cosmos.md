# Cosmos
### Welcome to the universe of ease and simplicity
Welcome, This is Cosmos a Harmony client made to look cool but also be easier to use
Accompanied its glassy and outer-space inspired look fuction and form have never worked together so well
Harmony can have its complicating moments so Cosmos is meant to make your experience easier 
With features still on the way there is still work to be done so be sure to look out for a new version
Feel free to give me some of your ideaa that could make Cosmos better using the Contact me button in the info page of Cosmos
